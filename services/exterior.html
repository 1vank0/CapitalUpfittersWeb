Why Exterior Accessories

ARMOR YOUR EXTERIOR.

The right exterior accessories protect your investment and finish your truck's look. Here's what they actually do.

Paint Protection

Fender flares and mud flaps stop rocks, road spray, and debris from damaging your paint. A $300 mud flap install can prevent a $1,500 paint repair.

Front-End Defense

Grille guards and bull bars protect your radiator, headlights, and front bumper from deer strikes, parking lot dings, and low-speed impacts.

Custom Look

The right exterior accessories complete your truck's look. Stock bumpers look stock. A ranch-style front bumper or aggressive fender flares make a statement.

OE-style fender flares have minimal aerodynamic impact since they follow the factory body contour. Cut-out/wide-body flares have a slightly larger profile but the difference in fuel economy is negligible for most drivers.
