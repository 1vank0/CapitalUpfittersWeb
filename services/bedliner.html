Why Patriot Liner

BUILT TO TAKE A BEATING.

Not all spray liners are the same. The chemistry, application temperature, and pressure make all the difference. Here's why Patriot Liner leads the field.

Lifetime Warranty

Every Patriot Liner installation comes with a lifetime manufacturer warranty. If it peels, fades, or cracks, we make it right — no questions asked.

Applied at 155°F & 2,000 PSI

Our heated, high-pressure application bonds the polyurea directly to the metal. It doesn't sit on top of your bed — it becomes part of it. Zero risk of delamination.

American-Made by Veterans

Patriot Liner is an American-made product developed and backed by veterans. You're not just protecting your truck — you're supporting a company built on integrity.

More Than a Truck Bed

PATRIOT LINER GOES ANYWHERE.

Polyurea spray coating bonds to almost any surface — metal, wood, concrete, fiberglass. If it needs protection, grip, or waterproofing, we can coat it.

Trailer Beds & Floors

Utility trailers, enclosed cargo trailers, flatbeds, horse trailers, boat trailers. Full waterproofing, rust protection, and non-slip grip.

Steps & Running Boards

Non-slip textured coating on truck steps, running boards, dock steps, industrial platforms. Grip that stays grippy even when wet.

Boats & Marine

Gunwales, decks, dock boards, pontoon floors. Polyurea is 100% waterproof and UV-stable — built for constant exposure to sun and water.

Garage & Workshop Floors

Concrete garage floors, shop floors, loading docks. Seamless, chemical-resistant coating that holds up to oil, solvents, and heavy equipment.

Cargo Vans & Work Trucks

Van floors, cargo areas, flatbed decks. Quieter ride, protected cargo, easy hose-out cleaning. Popular with contractors and delivery fleets.

Custom & Industrial

Playground equipment, ATVs, tool chests, dumpsters, agricultural equipment. If it can be sandblasted or prepped, it can be lined. Call us.

Our Process

FROM DROP-OFF TO PICKUP.

A clean, professional process every time. Your truck is treated with care from the moment it arrives.

1
Inspect & Assess

We inspect the bed for rust, damage, or accessories that need to be removed prior to application.

2
Surface Prep

The bed is cleaned, sanded, and masked. Proper prep ensures maximum adhesion and a clean edge line.

3
Spray Application

Patriot Liner is applied at 155°F, 2,000 PSI. Dries in under 10 seconds for a seamless, permanent bond.

4
Cure & Pickup

Your truck is ready in 2–3 hours. We do a final inspection before you pick it up, warranty registered.
