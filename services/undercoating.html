Why Undercoat

MARYLAND ROADS EAT METAL.

The DMV gets heavy salt treatment every winter. That salt works into every seam, every crevice, every surface your vehicle touches the road with. Undercoating is the only reliable defense.

Maryland Road Salt Protection

Road salt accelerates corrosion dramatically. Our undercoating creates a sealed barrier between your vehicle's metal and the caustic salt and moisture that come with every winter.

Permanent Rubberized Barrier

Rubberized undercoating bonds to metal and cures into a thick, flexible shield that doesn't crack, peel, or wash away. Applied right, it lasts the life of the vehicle.

Deadens Road Noise

A welcome side effect: rubberized undercoating acts as a sound damper. Many customers notice a noticeably quieter ride after undercoating — especially on highway surfaces.

Our Process

THOROUGH EVERY TIME.

1
Undercarriage Inspection

We put the vehicle on a lift and inspect for existing rust, damage, and any areas needing special attention before treatment.

2
Prep & Clean

The undercarriage is cleaned and dried. Rust neutralizer is applied where needed. Exhaust and moving parts are masked off.

3
Coating Application

Rubberized or oil-based undercoat is applied with professional equipment for full, even coverage — including frame rails and body seams.

4
Cure & Pickup

The coating sets quickly. Most vehicles are ready same-day. We do a final inspection before you take it home.
