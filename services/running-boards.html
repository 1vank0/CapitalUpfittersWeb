Our Process

BOLTED ON CLEAN.

1
Vehicle Lookup

We confirm your exact year, make, model, and cab configuration to select the right bracket hardware and step product.

2
Fitment Check

We verify the rocker mounting points and check for any accessories (lift kits, flares) that may require specific hardware or offsets.

3
Professional Install

Torqued to spec using factory-compatible hardware. Wiring harness connected for power steps. No drilling, no damage.

4
Level & Pickup

Final alignment check, test of operation (power steps), and delivery. Most installs take 1–2 hours.

Nerf bars are round or oval tubes that run along the rocker panel — they look rugged and provide a small step. Running boards are flat, wider platforms that give significantly more standing surface.
