WHY A RANCH CAMPER SHELL?

Not all caps are created equal. Ranch fiberglass shells are engineered to fit your specific truck — not just bolted on and hoped for the best.

Fully lockable fiberglass shell with weatherproof seals. Keeps your cargo dry, secure, and out of sight. Far more secure than a tonneau cover.

Looks Factory Installed

Ranch shells are painted to match your truck's factory color codes. Seamless fit means it looks like it came from the dealer.

Versatile Storage

Perfect for contractors, campers, dog owners, and anyone who needs a dry, secure cargo area. Add a bed liner and van shelving for a complete mobile workspace.

Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know before choosing a camper shell.

A camper shell (also called a truck cap or topper) is a full enclosure that sits on top of your bed rails, fully enclosing your cargo area. A tonneau cover is a flat panel that covers only the bed opening. Shells offer much more security, weather protection, and usable vertical space.

Yes — Ranch shells are available in factory color-match finishes for Ford, RAM, Chevrolet/GMC, Toyota, and Nissan. We use your truck's color code to order the exact match.

Most installs are same-day or next-day. The shell bolts to your bed rails with a weatherstrip seal — installation takes about 1–2 hours.

We keep popular models in stock for the most common truck sizes. Call (301) 304-1419 to check availability for your specific truck.

Yes — we recommend adding a Patriot Liner spray-on bedliner when installing a shell. Interior lighting, bed slides, and van shelving can also be added for a complete mobile workspace setup.
