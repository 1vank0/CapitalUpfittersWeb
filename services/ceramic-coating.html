Why Ceramic

PROTECTION THAT'S ACTUALLY PERMANENT.

The difference between a $20 spray and a professional ceramic coating is the difference between a bandage and surgery. One actually solves the problem.

Certified Installer (IGL + System X)

We're certified by two of the world's leading ceramic coating brands. That means proper prep, correct product selection, and a warranty you can actually use.

Hydrophobic Protection

Water beads and rolls off your paint instantly. Road grime, bird droppings, and tree sap can't chemically bond to a properly coated surface. Your paint stays cleaner, longer.

Self-Cleaning Effect

The nano-ceramic layer's high surface tension means rain water picks up loose dirt on the way down. Less washing, less swirling, less effort maintaining your paint.

Our Process

PREP IS EVERYTHING.

A ceramic coating is only as good as the prep work beneath it. We don't skip steps — and we don't coat over problems.

1
Paint Assessment

We inspect the paint under professional lighting for swirls, contamination, and oxidation to determine what prep is required.

2
Decontamination & Prep

Wash, clay bar, iron decontamination. Paint correction if needed. The surface must be perfect before any coating goes on.

3
Ceramic Application

Applied panel by panel in a controlled environment. Each layer is leveled and inspected before moving to the next panel.

4
Cure & Pickup

The coating cures for 24–48 hours. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and register your warranty before pickup.

FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

A professionally applied ceramic coating typically lasts 3–5 years with proper maintenance. Some premium coatings we apply are rated for 7+ years. Longevity depends on the product tier, how thorough the prep was, and how you maintain the vehicle afterward. We'll walk you through everything at pickup.

Surface decontamination is included in all pricing. Paint correction (polishing out swirls, scratches, and oxidation) is quoted separately based on the paint's actual condition — we assess before we quote. We won't coat over defects, which is why some shops do and their results don't last.

Wax sits on top of your paint and washes away within weeks. Ceramic coating chemically bonds to the clear coat, creating a permanent hydrophobic layer that repels water, blocks UV radiation, and resists chemical contamination from bird droppings, road salt, and tree sap. It lasts years — not weeks.

You should avoid water contact for 5–7 days after application while the coating fully cures. After that, washing becomes easier — the hydrophobic surface causes water and dirt to slide right off. We'll give you a detailed aftercare sheet when you pick up the vehicle.
