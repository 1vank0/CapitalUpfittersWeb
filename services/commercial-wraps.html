Why Wraps Work

8 REASONS TO WRAP YOUR FLEET.

Moving Billboards

Commercial vehicle wraps turn every mile your fleet drives into direct brand exposure — reaching thousands of people daily at zero ongoing cost.

Cost-Effective Advertising

One-time investment, 5–7 years of continuous advertising. No monthly fees, no ad spend, no algorithm changes. Vehicle wrap advertising delivers the lowest CPM of any media format.

Paint Protection

Premium vinyl acts as a protective barrier against UV rays, minor scratches, stone chips, and environmental damage. When removed, original paint is preserved — protecting fleet resale value.

Brand Consistency

Every vehicle in your fleet presents an identical, professional brand identity. Consistent colors, logo placement, and messaging across 1 or 100 vehicles.

Customizable Design

High-resolution full-color graphics, custom color changes, or targeted lettering. We work from your brand guidelines or start from scratch with our design team.

Fast Turnaround

Most installations complete in 1–3 business days. Vinyl lettering is often same-day. Batch fleet scheduling keeps your vehicles out of service for minimum time.

Professional Appearance

A uniformly branded fleet signals that your business is established, organized, and serious. Customers form impressions before you say a word — make it count.

Long-Lasting Durability

3M and Avery Dennison cast vinyl is rated for outdoor UV exposure, weather resistance, and commercial use. Properly installed and maintained wraps last 5–7 years in the DMV climate.

How It Works

FROM FIRST CALL TO BRANDED FLEET.

We make the process straightforward — design, approve, install. Most wrap projects go from first contact to finished install in under two weeks.

01
Consultation & Quote

Call or submit a quote online. We discuss your vehicle(s), brand goals, and coverage level. Quote delivered within 24 hours — usually same business day.

02
Design & Approval

Provide your logo (vector file), brand colors, and contact info. We deliver an initial design concept within 48 hours. One-on-one revisions until you're 100% satisfied before we print anything.

03
Print & Install

Once design is approved, we print on 3M or Avery Dennison cast vinyl and schedule your installation. Most vehicles are wrapped in 1–3 business days by our professional installers.

04
Quality Check & Pickup

We inspect every panel before releasing the vehicle. Your wrap comes with a 1-year workmanship warranty. Maintenance tips provided. Fleet accounts tracked via Upfit Portal.

What to bring: High-resolution vector logo file (.ai, .eps, or .svg) • Brand color codes (Pantone or hex) • Contact info for the graphics • Any specific design preferences
